We are a Kadoma based School focused on excellence.

"Excellence in education is when we do everything we can to make sure they become everything they can.", to us excellence is not simply an accomplishment but it's a spirit, a never-ending process we strive for everyday in class and outside.

At Brookside School we imagine every learner to be a project under construction and thus we give it our best of focus and attention for it is a future essential.

Why Choose Brookside Schools

Below we show you why amongst all local schools you should choose to educate your child with us.

Legality and formality

All of our Brookside learning facilities are recognized and encouraged by the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education.

School Values. ...

We value importing knowledge that leads to the upliftment of children without them relying on adults. We also value high renown honor won by notable achievements

Low student to teacher ratio

Our class populations are limited to 20 or below learners to one teacher. This sufficient ratio enables our learners to get full attention and guidance from the teacher.

International Curriculum

We educate our learners on a Cambridge curriculum. This gives our learners the choice to seek tetiary education or employment abroad.

Qualified Teachers

Our teachers are experts in the education sector. Our teachers have been grooming bright learners and will continue grooming innovative and wise citizens.

Location

We are located in the heart of Kadoma, this serves as a central destination for our learners from different parts of Kadoma.
